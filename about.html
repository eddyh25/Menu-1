About Grand Hôtel Bois de Boulogne

Welcome

Nestled in pine-forested grounds of around 24,000 m² in Dhour El Choueir at approximately 1,200 m elevation, Grand Hôtel Bois de Boulogne offers a peaceful mountain escape just 30–40 km from Beirut and the international airport. Conveniently located between Beirut and the Bekaa Valley, it is only 8 km from Zaarour ski slopes.

History & Character

Established in the 1930s and later renovated, the hotel blends classic elegance with modern comfort. With its rustic charm, spacious rooms, and refined ambiance, it provides the perfect retreat for both leisure and business travelers.

Guest Rooms & Accommodation

The hotel features 78–100 guest rooms and 12 suites/apartments, some with separate living spaces or kitchens—ideal for families or long stays. Guests particularly appreciate the quiet atmosphere, spacious rooms, and comfortable beds.
